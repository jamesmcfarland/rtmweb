WHO ARE WE?

BY RICH TEA ADMINS

Rich Tea Media is the umbrella for three main products; the Discord Server Rich Tea Radio, The weekly music livesteam Rich Tea Live, and the podcast Rich Tea On Air. We are based in the UK and made up of a team of 8 creative minds with new ideas and thoughts to take us further. Our team is a mix of Gamers, Coders, Artists and a Farmer who love what they do in their roles. So lets meet our crack team!
